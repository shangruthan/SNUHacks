Job Title: Software Development Engineer (SDE)

Company Overview:

At Standard Chartered, we are a leading international bank with a rich history and a strong presence in emerging markets. We are committed to innovation and excellence, and we are seeking a talented and motivated Software Development Engineer to join our dynamic team. As an SDE, you will play a pivotal role in designing, developing, and maintaining cutting-edge cloud-based software solutions that drive our business forward.

Job Description:

We are looking for a detail-oriented and creative Software Development Engineer to help build and enhance our suite of cloud-based applications. In this role, you will be responsible for writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code while collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver exceptional software solutions. The ideal candidate is not only technically proficient but also excels at problem-solving, communication, and teamwork.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and Development: Design, develop, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code across the full stack.

Software Development Lifecycle: Participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle, from ideation to deployment and beyond.

Collaboration: Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers to understand requirements and translate them into technical solutions.

APIs and Microservices: Build and deploy RESTful APIs and microservices to support scalable application architectures.

Front-end Development: Implement responsive and accessible front-end interfaces using modern frameworks like React.js.

Database Management: Develop and optimize database schemas, ensuring data integrity and performance.

Security Measures: Implement robust security measures, including authentication, authorization, and data protection protocols.

Optimization: Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and reliability.

Troubleshooting: Debug complex issues, monitor performance, and troubleshoot production environments.

Staying Updated: Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve our systems and processes.

Requirements:

Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical field (Master’s degree preferred but not mandatory).

Experience: Minimum 3 years of professional software development experience, with a proven track record of working in Agile development environments.

Technical Skills: Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, and JavaScript, along with experience in frameworks like Spring Boot, React.js, and Node.js.

Database Knowledge: Strong knowledge of database technologies, including SQL (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB).

Cloud Experience: Hands-on experience with cloud platforms like AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda) or Azure, as well as CI/CD pipelines and version control tools like Git.

Scalability: Demonstrated ability to design and implement scalable, high-performance applications and RESTful APIs.

Testing: Experience with unit testing, integration testing, and test automation to ensure code quality.

Communication Skills: Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to debug complex technical issues. Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively in a team setting.

Preferred Qualifications:

Open-source Contributions: Contributions to open-source projects or experience with performance monitoring tools.

Certifications: Certifications such as AWS Certified Developer, Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate, or Oracle Certified Professional, Java SE Programmer are highly desirable.

What We Offer:

Competitive Compensation: Competitive salary and bonus structure.

Benefits: Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.

401(k) Matching: 401(k) matching program to support your financial future.

Professional Development: Professional development opportunities, including access to training and certifications.

Work-Life Balance: Flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance.

Inclusive Environment: A collaborative and inclusive work environment where innovation thrives.
