Job Description: Software Development Engineer (SDE)
Company Overview:
Our rapidly growing technology company is seeking a talented and motivated Software Development Engineer (SDE) to join our innovative team. As an SDE, you will play a pivotal role in designing, developing, and maintaining cutting-edge cloud-based software solutions that drive our business forward. If you are passionate about building scalable, high-performance applications and thrive in a collaborative, fast-paced environment, we want to hear from you!

Job Description:
We are looking for a detail-oriented and creative Software Development Engineer to help build and enhance our suite of cloud-based applications. In this role, you will be responsible for writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code while collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver exceptional software solutions. The ideal candidate is not only technically proficient but also excels at problem-solving, communication, and teamwork.

Key Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain efficient, reusable, and reliable code across the full stack.
Participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle, from ideation to deployment and beyond.
Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers to understand requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
Build and deploy RESTful APIs and microservices to support scalable application architectures.
Implement responsive and accessible front-end interfaces using modern frameworks like React.js.
Develop and optimize database schemas, ensuring data integrity and performance.
Implement robust security measures, including authentication, authorization, and data protection protocols.
Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and reliability.
Debug complex issues, monitor performance, and troubleshoot production environments.
Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve our systems and processes.
Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical field (Master’s degree preferred but not mandatory).
Minimum 3 years of professional software development experience, with a proven track record of working in Agile development environments.
Proficiency in programming languages such as Java, Python, and JavaScript , along with experience in frameworks like Spring Boot, React.js, and Node.js .
Strong knowledge of database technologies, including SQL (PostgreSQL/MySQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB) .
Hands-on experience with cloud platforms like AWS (EC2, S3, Lambda) or Azure , as well as CI/CD pipelines and version control tools like Git .
Demonstrated ability to design and implement scalable, high-performance applications and RESTful APIs.
Experience with unit testing, integration testing, and test automation to ensure code quality.
Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to debug complex technical issues.
Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively in a team setting.
Preferred Qualifications:

Contributions to open-source projects or experience with performance monitoring tools.
Certifications such as AWS Certified Developer , Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate , or Oracle Certified Professional, Java SE Programmer are highly desirable.
What We Offer:

Competitive salary and bonus structure.
Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance.
401(k) matching program to support your financial future.
Professional development opportunities, including access to training and certifications.
Flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance.
A collaborative and inclusive work environment where innovation thrives.
